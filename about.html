About Quantum File Forge

Our Mission: Privacy-First Utility

Quantum File Forge was developed to solve a common problem: the security risk of uploading sensitive documents to remote servers for simple conversion tasks. We believe that your data belongs to you. That is why our platform utilizes advanced client-side JavaScript to process every file directly within your browser's memory.

Browser-Based Processing

By leveraging technologies like WebAssembly and libraries such as pdf-lib and jsPDF, we perform heavy-duty file manipulations without a backend server. This ensures zero data latency and 100% privacy.

Iterative Optimization

Our "Target Size" tool uses a unique recursive algorithm that adjusts rendering quality in steps (90% down to 10%) to help users meet strict file-size requirements for government and legal portals.

Why Choose Our Forge?

  • No Upload Limits: Since the processing happens on your hardware, we don't restrict file counts.
  • Zero Data Retention: We cannot see, store, or sell your files because they never reach our servers.
  • Precision Control: Specify your exact target KB, and our engine will find the best quality-to-size ratio for you.
